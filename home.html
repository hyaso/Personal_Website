Hometown

I was born in Detroit, Michigan and raised in my hometown of Dearborn, Michigan. Dearborn is the seventh most-populated city in Michigan and is home to the largest Muslim population in the USA per capita. It is also the birthplace and hometown of Henry Ford, the founder of the Ford Motor Company. It is known for its diversity, with many of its residents being descendants of 19th and 20th century immigrants. For more information on my hometown, check out this wikipedia page: Dearborn, Michigan.
