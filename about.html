About Me

My name is Hussein Yassine and I am from Dearborn, Michigan. Earlier this year, I decided to try and learn a bit of code. After taking the Harvard CS50 course online, I decided to fully enroll in a programming bootcamp through Grand Circus and hopefully become a full-blown software developer. I am about 3 months into my journey and have been enjoying myself so far. There has been many moments where I have just wanted to give up due to the frustations I've encountered while learning to program, but here I am still coding along. The satisfaction I get when a program I've been working on finally works is what makes all this worthwhile. Although I am still fairly new to programming, I believe that with putting in the time and effort I can one day become a senior level developer and turn this into a career.
